Where is tungsten/ Tungsten Carbide used in our life?

Most things made of tungsten are actually made with tungsten alloys, which are mixtures of tungsten with other metals. For instance, tungsten alloyed with carbon forms incredibly hard tungsten carbide.
The pure metal tungsten is in many more places in our lives than we realize. Since tungsten is such a rigid and strengthen metal, it is a benefit for golfers to make the face of the drive out of tungsten. This helps weight down the driver and out the center of gravity lower to the ground. Talking about the rigidity of tungsten, it is also a very good use for drilling through tough materials and it will be durable for a longer period of time compared to steel drill bits. For armor piercing penetration, making a bullet stronger by making the shell out of tungsten creates a huge improvement for the bullet. Finally, a tungsten ring is a more casual use for the precious metal. It's almost as heavy as gold, so tungsten carbide rings have a satisfying “heft” when worn. Many people enjoy the sleek design of the ring and the heft giving with it. 

How Our lives Would change if tungsten was never found
In this electronic age, the vacuum tube is the heart of virtually all devices. The filaments in this tube must have extremely high heat resistance. Tungsten in the pure metal form fills the bill as no other substance does and at a cost that is in reach with anyone. Without such efficient filaments, we would have no electric lamps, electric tubes and may more amazing electronic devices. 
Without tungsten, industry's and manufacture, transportation and communications as we know them today as Canada could've not existed. The freedom and and the vast amounts of necessities we have today due to the fact we wouldn't be able to vastly produce the equipment. Even if we were able to produce these equipment, it would cost much more than it is right now. Since tungsten is such strong metal and has a high melting point, we use it in most of our heavy industrial projects. From creating machinery to even help stabilize houses, the mining of this metal was an amazing choice.
